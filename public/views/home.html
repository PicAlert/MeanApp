PicAlert

Better alerts, directly to you

PicAlert is an inexpensive, easy-to-use home security system. Simply set up the device, register to our website, and you will be able to monitor any disturbances in your home while you are out and about. The device will send a picture directly to your phone whenever it detects movement in the house.

Easy to use.

Protect your home in a matter of minutes. Here's how to do it.

Register your device online.

Simply register an account on our website. Input the device serial and pin that came with your device and register your phone number. This will connect the device to your phone so that you can immediately receive alerts from anywhere.

Place and connect device.

Running the device is as simple switching it on, and mounting it around the house. The device will automatically sense movement when you are not home, and take a picture of who is creeping through your house.

Get alerts directly to you.

By activating the device when you are not home, you will be instantly notified whether you are at work, at the gym, or anywhere in the world with an LG connection.

Affordable

Stop paying hundreds of dollars every month.

The PicAlert device has a one-time fee at an affordable price. You will only be charged everytime at alert is sent, which cost as much as a text message.
